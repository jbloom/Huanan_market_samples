Correlation of read counts aligned to mitochondria of different species in current study vs Crits-Christoph et al

For each sequencing run analyzed by Crits-Christoph et al (2023) , this plot shows the Pearson correlation in the counts of aligned mitochondrial reads in the current study versus Table S3 of Crits-Christoph et al across all species in that table.

You can mouseover the points for details, and use the sliders at the bottom of the plot to adjust how many total aligned mitochondrial reads in each study are required for a run in order for it to be shown in this plot.

See Bloom et al, bioRxiv, DOI 10.1101/2023.04.25.538336 for a paper describing this analysis.
